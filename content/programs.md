from a focused training environment and live in modern boarding facilities. Mornings

discipline, resilience, and life skills both on and off the course. The program

## 3. Advanced Golf Program — Grades 9–12

**Schedule:** Monday–Saturday

**Overview:**
The Advanced Golf Program at Chiang Rai International School is a full-time,
high-performance boarding program for student-athletes in Grades 9–12 who are
committed to excellence both in the classroom and on the course.

Based at Happy City Golf Club, this unique residential program provides a holistic
development experience that nurtures the mind, body, and character of each student.
It integrates elite golf training, a rigorous international academic curriculum, and
a structured lifestyle that prepares young athletes for the demands of college golf
or a professional pathway.

**Mandatory Boarding** — all students live at Happy City Golf Club's residential
facilities, allowing for:
- Daily access to top-tier golf training and fitness resources
- Supervised routines that support healthy sleep, nutrition, and recovery
- Strong peer community of like-minded athletes
- Time management and independence skills that are essential for college and life
